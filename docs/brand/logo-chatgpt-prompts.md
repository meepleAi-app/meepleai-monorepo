# MeepleAI Logo — ChatGPT/DALL-E Prompts

> Session: 2026-03-27
> Status: In progress — iterating on colors and hex frame

## Design Brief (da brainstorming)

- **Forma**: Meeple autentico (sagoma Carcassonne)
- **Occhi**: Trattini orizzontali (— —), stile zen/consapevole
- **Corona**: Piccola, dorata, inclinata sulla testa
- **Glow**: Bordo luminoso, sparkle effects
- **Sfondo**: Esagonale (non rounded square)
- **Concept tipografico**: Il meeple = la "A" di Meeple**A**i
- **Feel**: Playful + tech-smart + community (Discord vibes)
- **Uso**: Primarily digital (favicon/PWA/webapp), secondary physical (sticker/merch)
- **Reference approvato**: `ChatGPT Image 27 mar 2026, 15_26_06.png` (viola con corona, glow, dash eyes)

## Prompt che ha generato il reference approvato

```
Flat vector logo of a board game meeple piece (the classic Carcassonne-style pawn shape) in indigo/purple gradient (#6366f1 to #8b5cf6). The meeple has two minimal horizontal line eyes (like — —) giving it a zen, knowing expression. A small tilted golden crown sits on its head.
```
