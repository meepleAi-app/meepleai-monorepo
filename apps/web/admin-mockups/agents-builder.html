What are AI Agents?

AI agents are specialized assistants with domain expertise, custom personalities, and specific capabilities. They help users by answering questions, providing guidance, and solving domain-specific problems.

  • Rules clarification and interpretation
  • Strategic gameplay advice
  • Game recommendations based on preferences
  • Custom scenarios and challenges

Quick Start Guide

  1. Define Purpose What will your agent specialize in?
  2. Set Personality Tone, style, and interaction approach
  3. Configure Capabilities Knowledge domains and tools access
  4. Test in Playground Validate behavior before publishing
  5. Publish & Monitor Track usage and gather feedback
